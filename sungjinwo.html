"Solo Leveling" is a popular Korean web novel written by Chugong,
which was later adapted into a webtoon (manhwa) and eventually an anime.
It follows Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest E-rank hunter in a world
where humanity fights against monsters in dungeons.
